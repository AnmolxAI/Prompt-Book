## 1. Product Strategy Clarity Prompt

**Prompt:**  
Act as a senior product strategist. I will describe my product, its users, and the problem I’m solving. Break it down into:  
1. Core problem statement  
2. JTBD  
3. Value hypothesis  
4. Risks & assumptions  
5. Three strategic options  
6. What a V1 should and should not include.

---

## 2. PRD Deep-Dive Prompt

**Prompt:**  
Turn the following idea into a complete PRD with: problem, context, scope, user stories, acceptance criteria, constraints, risks, UX flows, KPIs, and an engineering handoff section.  
Ask 5 clarifying questions first.

---

## 3. Competitive Benchmarking Prompt

**Prompt:**  
You are a competitive intelligence analyst. Compare the following products across: positioning, key features, gaps, pricing, business model, onboarding experience, and switching costs.  
Then produce:  
1. Differentiation map  
2. Moat analysis  
3. Actionable opportunities

---

## 4. Roadmap Prioritization Prompt

**Prompt:**  
Given my backlog items below, use RICE, MoSCoW, and 2x2 value/effort frameworks to prioritize them.  
Create a 90-day roadmap and identify dependencies, risks, and cross-functional requirements.

---

## 5. User Research → Insight Prompt

**Prompt:**  
I’ll paste user research transcripts. Convert them into insights by grouping themes, pain points, unmet needs, motivations, behavioral patterns, and contradictions.  
Then produce: personas, JTBD, opportunity areas, and top 5 product bets.
